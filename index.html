Instructions: How to Play?

The game is simple and easy to understand.

  1. When you click on continue you will go to game page.
  2. You will be having a input box to enter your guess.
  3. Emojis will be there and you need to guess the movie names based on the emojis displayed
  4. When movie name you guessed is right then it will increase your score
  5. When your guess is wrong game ends up and you will get a popup with game over messages
  6. If You are interested u can play the game again by pressing play-again button
