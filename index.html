Instructions: How to Play?

The game is simple and easy to understand.

  1. When you click on continue you will go to game page.
  2. Players need to roll the dice in-order to play the game[Dice will have numbers from 1 -6].
  3. Then roll the dice by clicking on "Roll Dice". You can see the number on it in real time.
  4. When player gets 6 on the roll he gets the 2nd chance to roll the dice,else the other player gets a chance to play
  5. At few tiles the board will have snake or ladder, when ladder present player goes up in the board
  6. When Snake is there then player comes back to the tip of the snake
  7. The player who reaches the top first will be the winner
